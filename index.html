About School

Middle School, Gomhar is a government school with around 200–250 students. Both boys and girls study here in a safe and friendly environment. The school ensures that all educational facilities and government benefits are provided to students on time. With dedicated teachers and active learners, the school is recognized as one of the leading schools in the area.
